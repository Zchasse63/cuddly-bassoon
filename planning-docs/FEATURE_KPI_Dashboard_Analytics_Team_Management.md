# Feature Idea: KPI Dashboard & Analytics System

**Category:** Platform Infrastructure / Team Management / User Insights  
**Priority:** Future Enhancement (Critical for Teams)  
**Status:** Research & Planning  
**Last Updated:** December 2, 2025

---

## Overview

Provide users with comprehensive analytics on their platform activity, deal performance, and improvement trends. Scale from individual snapshots to full team management dashboards with admin controls. Give users the data they need to optimize their wholesaling business.

---

## Core Philosophy

> "What gets measured gets managed."

Our analytics should:
1. **Inform** - Show users exactly what they're doing
2. **Compare** - Benchmark against past performance and peers
3. **Guide** - Suggest improvements based on data
4. **Motivate** - Celebrate wins and progress
