# Skip Trace & Enrichment Tools

## Overview

Tools for finding contact information and enriching lead data.

**Total Tools**: 10

## Available Tools


### skipTraceLead

**ID**: `skip_trace.trace_lead`

Skip trace a lead to find phone numbers, emails, and addresses


### batchSkipTrace

**ID**: `skip_trace.batch_trace`

Queue multiple leads for skip tracing


### getSkipTraceStatus

**ID**: `skip_trace.get_status`

Check the status of a skip trace request


### validatePhoneNumber

**ID**: `skip_trace.validate_phone`

Validate and get info about a phone number


### validateEmail

**ID**: `skip_trace.validate_email`

Validate an email address


### enrichLeadData

**ID**: `skip_trace.enrich_lead`

Enrich lead with demographics, property, or financial data


### findRelatedContacts

**ID**: `skip_trace.find_related`

Find relatives, associates, or neighbors of a lead


### reversePhoneLookup

**ID**: `skip_trace.reverse_phone`

Look up owner information by phone number


### reverseAddressLookup

**ID**: `skip_trace.reverse_address`

Look up owner information by property address


### getSkipTraceCredits

**ID**: `skip_trace.get_credits`

Check remaining skip trace credits


## Common Use Cases

- Find phone numbers and emails
- Validate contact info
- Enrich lead records
