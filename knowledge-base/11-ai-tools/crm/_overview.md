# CRM & Lead Tools

## Overview

Tools for managing leads, tracking engagement, and organizing your pipeline.

**Total Tools**: 12

## Available Tools


### createLeadList

**ID**: `crm.create_lead_list`

Create a new lead list for organizing leads


### rankListByMotivation

**ID**: `crm.rank_by_motivation`

Rank leads by motivation score, highest first


### suggestLeadOutreach

**ID**: `crm.suggest_outreach`

Suggest leads to contact based on motivation and status


### analyzeLeadSource

**ID**: `crm.analyze_source`

Analyze performance of different lead sources


### segmentLeads

**ID**: `crm.segment_leads`

Segment leads by motivation, status, source, or recency


### predictLeadConversion

**ID**: `crm.predict_conversion`

Predict likelihood of lead converting to deal


### generateLeadReport

**ID**: `crm.generate_report`

Generate a summary report for leads


### identifyHotLeads

**ID**: `crm.identify_hot`

Find leads with high motivation scores


### trackLeadEngagement

**ID**: `crm.track_engagement`

Track engagement history for a lead


### suggestLeadNurturing

**ID**: `crm.suggest_nurturing`

Suggest nurturing plan for a lead


### mergeLeadRecords

**ID**: `crm.merge_leads`

Merge duplicate lead records


### exportLeadData

**ID**: `crm.export_leads`

Export lead data in JSON or CSV format


## Common Use Cases

- Organize leads into lists
- Track engagement history
- Prioritize outreach
