# Market Research Workflow

## Overview

Before investing in a new market, thorough research helps identify opportunities and risks. This workflow guides you through comprehensive market analysis.

## Step 1: Get Market Overview

### Request Basic Market Data
```
"Give me a market overview for [city/ZIP code]"
"What's the real estate market like in Tampa, FL?"
```

### Key Metrics to Review
| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| Median Price | Price point you'll be working in |
| Price Trend (YoY) | Market direction |
| Days on Market | How fast properties sell |
| Inventory | Supply level |
| Sale-to-List Ratio | Seller vs buyer market |

## Step 2: Analyze Price Trends

### Request Historical Data
```
"Show price trends for [market] over the last 2 years"
"How have home values changed in this area?"
```

### Interpret Price Trends
| YoY Change | Market Type | Strategy |
|------------|-------------|----------|
| > +10% | Hot/Appreciating | Act fast, competitive |
| +5-10% | Growing | Strong fundamentals |
| 0-5% | Stable | Conservative approach |
| < 0% | Declining | Extra caution, deeper discounts |

## Step 3: Assess Market Velocity

### Request Velocity Metrics
```
"What's the market velocity in [ZIP code]?
Show DOM, inventory, and absorption rate"
```

### Velocity Analysis
**Days on Market (DOM):**
| DOM | Market Speed | Implication |
|-----|--------------|-------------|
| < 15 | Very Fast | Quick exits, but competitive |
| 15-30 | Fast | Good for wholesaling |
| 30-60 | Normal | Standard approach |
| 60-90 | Slow | Need deeper discounts |
| > 90 | Very Slow | High risk for wholesaling |

**Months of Inventory:**
| Supply | Market Type |
|--------|-------------|
| < 3 months | Seller's market |
| 3-6 months | Balanced |
| > 6 months | Buyer's market |

## Step 4: Study Demographics

### Request Demographic Data
```
"Show demographics for [market]:
- Population and growth
- Income levels
- Age distribution
- Owner vs renter ratio"
```

### Key Demographic Indicators
| Factor | Positive Signs | Warning Signs |
|--------|----------------|---------------|
| Population | Growing > 1%/yr | Declining |
| Income | Rising, > regional avg | Falling |
| Age | Mix, strong 25-45 | Very old or young |
| Ownership | > 60% owner-occupied | < 40% owner-occupied |

## Step 5: Analyze Property Mix

### Request Property Data
```
"What's the property type breakdown in [market]?
Show by type, age, and price range"
```

### Understand Property Distribution
| Factor | Analysis |
|--------|----------|
| Property Types | SFR vs Multi vs Condo |
| Age Distribution | New vs established vs old |
| Price Tiers | Entry, mid, upper market |
| Condition | Updated vs deferred maintenance |

## Step 6: Identify Opportunity Indicators

### Request Motivation Signals
```
"What percentage of properties in [market] have:
- Absentee owners
- High equity
- Long-term ownership
- Out-of-state owners"
```

### Market Opportunity Score
| Indicator | Opportunity Level |
|-----------|-------------------|
| > 30% absentee | High opportunity |
| > 40% high equity | High opportunity |
| > 25% long ownership | Good equity buildup |
| > 15% out-of-state | Distance-motivated sellers |

## Step 7: Assess Competition

### Research Investor Activity
```
"Show investor activity in [market]:
- Cash buyer percentage
- Flip activity
- Rental investor presence"
```

### Competition Analysis
| Factor | Interpretation |
|--------|----------------|
| High cash buyer % | Active investor market |
| Many recent flips | Proven flip market |
| Strong rental demand | Multiple exit options |
| Few investors | Less competition |

## Step 8: Check Economic Factors

### Request Economic Data
```
"What are the economic indicators for [market]?
- Employment trends
- Major employers
- Economic diversification"
```

### Economic Health Indicators
| Factor | Positive | Negative |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Employment | Growing, diversified | Single employer dependent |
| Job Creation | New employers moving in | Employers leaving |
| Industries | Multiple sectors | Single industry |

## Step 9: Analyze Permit Activity

### Request Permit Data
```
"Show construction permit activity in [market]:
- New construction volume
- Renovation activity
- Permit trends"
```

### Permit Analysis
| Activity | Implication |
|----------|-------------|
| High new construction | Growing area |
| High renovation | Revitalizing area |
| Low permits, old homes | Potential for value-add |
| Declining permits | Slowing market |

## Market Research Checklist

Complete before entering a new market:

```
□ Market Overview
  □ Median price and trend
  □ Days on market
  □ Inventory level
  □ Price per square foot

□ Demographics
  □ Population trend
  □ Income levels
  □ Age distribution
  □ Owner vs renter

□ Opportunity Indicators
  □ Absentee owner percentage
  □ Equity levels
  □ Motivation signals

□ Competition Assessment
  □ Investor activity
  □ Cash buyer percentage
  □ Flip volume

□ Economic Factors
  □ Employment trends
  □ Major employers
  □ Economic diversity

□ Entry Strategy
  □ Target neighborhoods
  □ Property types
  □ Price points
```

## Generate Market Report
```
"Generate a complete market research report for [market] including:
- Market statistics and trends
- Demographic analysis
- Opportunity assessment
- Competition analysis
- Entry recommendation"
```

## Market Entry Decision Framework

### Go/No-Go Criteria
| Criteria | Go Signal | Caution | No-Go |
|----------|-----------|---------|-------|
| Price Trend | Rising | Flat | Declining > 5% |
| DOM | < 45 days | 45-75 days | > 75 days |
| Population | Growing | Stable | Declining |
| Absentee % | > 25% | 15-25% | < 15% |
| Competition | Moderate | High | None (no market) |
